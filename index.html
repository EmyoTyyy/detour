How to play

  • Each turn: move your pawn one step, or place a wall.
  • First pawn to reach the opposite row wins.
  • Walls go between cells and block movement. You start with 10.
  • A wall can't fully trap a player — both must keep a path home.
  • Jump a face-to-face opponent; side-step if a wall is behind them.
